Difference Between:


In this section, we will discuss about difference between most of the concept. SO, let's begin.


Customer Due Diligence vs Enhanced Due Diligence

Basis CDD EDD
Purpose To identify the entities (including IBO/s) and individuals associated with the company To verify the entities (including IBO/s) and individuals associated with the company
Frequency of Review Generally at the time of onboarding Once in a Year
Ownership Percentage 25% or more 10% or more
Applicability To all the customers as it is basic check Only to High risk customers
Documentation Minimal Documentation (only for identification purpose) High Level of Documentation (For verification purposes)

Source of Funds vs Source of Wealth

Basis Source of Funds Source of Wealth
Definition/Scope Origin of the funds for the specific transaction overall accumulation of a person's assets and wealth over time
Timeline Immediate Origin of funds of that specific transaction History of an individual's financial resources
Documentation Minimal Documentation as it is related to only specific transaction Comprehensive Documentation for in in-depth understanding of customer's wealth generation
Source of origin Personal Savings/Employment Income etc. Inheritence/Business Ownership etc.

Private Limited vs Limited Liability Partnership

Basis Private Limited Limited Liability Partnership
Legal Extension Pvt. Ltd. LLP
Act Companies Act LLP Act
Formation/Charter Document Memorandum and Article of Association LLP Agreement
Members 2 to 200 Members 2 partners to Unlimited
Liability Limited up to the value of their shares Limited up to their agreed contribution
Transfer or Ownership/Rights Easily Transferrable As per LLP agreement
Decision Maker Generally Director Designated Partner
Voting Rights Based on number of shares As per LLP agreement

Limited Liability Partnership vs Limited Partnership

Basis Limited Liability Partnership Limited Partnership
Legal Extension LLP LP
Liability Limited (No personal assets involved) General Partners have unlimited personal liability
Limited Partners have limited liability
Members 2 partners to unlimited partners 1 General Partner and 1 limited partner, no maximum limit
Transferibility As per LLP agreement Generally transferrable but with conditions
General Partner No General Partner Atleast 1 General Partner
Management and decision making General partner/s makes the decision and manages day to day activity All partners can involve in both day to day activities and decision making

Limited Liability Partnership vs General Partnership

Basis Limited Liability Partnership General Partnership
Legal Extension LLP Generally GP
Liability Limited (No personal assets involved) All Partners have unlimited personal liability
Transferibility As per LLP agreement Generally transferrable but with conditions
General Partner No General Partner Atleast 2 General Partner
Management and decision making All partners can involve in both day to day activities and decision making General partner/s makes the decision and manages day to day activity

Limited Partnership vs General Partnership

Basis Limited Partnership General Partnership
Legal Extension LP Generally GP
Liability Only Limited partner/s have unlimited personal liability, limited partners are liabile only to their investment All Partners have unlimited personal liability
General Partner Atleast 1 General Partner Atleast 2 General Partner
Management and decision making General partner/s makes the decision and manages day to day activity All partners can involve in both day to day activities and decision making
